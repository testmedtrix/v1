ABOUT US

Addressing the needs of Patients, Physicians, Pharma & Providers, the 4 Ps, forms the core of our existence. People, passion and process are the strengths that help us retain 80% of our clients and receive an average client satisfaction rate of 4.6 on a scale of 5, while also providing cost savings of about 47%.

Eight of the top 30 global pharmaceutical companies and one of the largest healthcare providers in the world partner with us. The reliability of ISO certified processes and dependability of timely delivery made 3 of the top 10 pharmaceutical companies entrust us with delivering services for their top brands.

Our global footprint, which spans 4 continents, has empowered our clients to leverage best business practices accepted all over world. We have established ISO 9001 and ISO 27001 certified processes across all aspects of operations that ensure highest quality of content delivered through innovative cutting-edge formats.

Scientific Event Support
